# llm_speak Eval Rubric (Starter)

Score each rewrite 1-5 on:

1. Intent Preservation
- Did meaning stay intact?
- Any invented claims or commitments?

2. Professional Clarity
- Is wording clear and unambiguous?
- Is the message easy to act on?

3. Tone Fit
- Does tone match target audience and preset?
- Is it too soft, too aggressive, or balanced?

4. Accountability Quality
- If owning a miss, is responsibility clear?
- Are next steps concrete and time-bound?

5. Brevity
- Is rewrite concise enough for chat/email?
- Any obvious filler that can be removed?

## Pass/Fail Conditions

Fail immediately if:

- facts are fabricated
- commitments are invented
- deceptive framing is introduced
- tone becomes insulting or manipulative

## Suggested Test Set

Start with 20 prompts:

- 5 admit-fault
- 5 pushback
- 4 deadline-shift
- 3 status-update
- 3 client-safe
