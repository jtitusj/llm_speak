# Skill: Push Back

## Goal

Disagree or challenge direction without sounding hostile or defensive.

## Rules

- Start with alignment signal (`I see your point`).
- Present concern as a tradeoff, not a rejection.
- Use evidence language where possible.
- Propose an alternative or a validation step.
- Keep tone firm but cooperative.

## Preferred Structure

1. Align briefly
2. Concern/tradeoff
3. Alternative proposal
4. Decision suggestion
