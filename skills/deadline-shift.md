# Skill: Deadline Shift

## Goal

Request more time while maintaining accountability and confidence.

## Rules

- Be explicit that original date will slip.
- Give a clear revised date/time window.
- Explain blocker in one short factual line.
- Include mitigation or recovery plan.
- Avoid vague wording like `maybe` or `soon`.

## Preferred Structure

1. Timeline update
2. Cause (brief)
3. Revised delivery date
4. Mitigation and checkpoint
