# Skill: Client-Safe Messaging

## Goal

Communicate clearly to clients with low-risk, trust-preserving language.

## Rules

- Avoid internal jargon or blame assignment.
- Do not speculate on root cause unless confirmed.
- State what is known, what is being done, and when next update arrives.
- Keep tone calm, professional, and reassuring.

## Preferred Structure

1. Acknowledge situation
2. Current known facts
3. Action underway
4. Next update time
